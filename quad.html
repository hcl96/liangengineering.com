Quadcopter Flight Control

How to Fly the Quadcopter?

In collaboration with IEEE Quadcopter Team, I helped design an optimal controller for a hovering quadcopter.
The linearized dynamics for quadcopter during hover is analyzed in MATLAB. A combination of LQR and Kalman filter is designed and simulated in SIMULINK for optimal performance. The controller is then implemented in C on a BeagleBone Blue with the help of Robot Control Library APIs to balance and fly the quadcopter
